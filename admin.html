Menu & Photos

Update prices, hide sold-out dishes, and replace any photo. Click Publish and it goes live for customers in about a minute.

How it works

  • Price — type a new price; it updates on the online-order page.
  • Type — mark a dish Veg or Non-veg (changes the dot on the menu).
  • Available — turn a dish off to hide it from customers (e.g. sold out). Turn it back on anytime.
  • Replace — upload a new photo for a dish. It’s resized automatically and updates everywhere that dish appears.
  • Site photos — replace non-dish images like the home-page hero.
  • Publish — saves everything and pushes it live. Customers see changes in ~1 minute (a browser refresh may be needed).

Nothing is live until you press Publish. You can make several edits and publish them together.
